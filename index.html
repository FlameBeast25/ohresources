Wish Machine

A lot of times people think that the gambling option in the wish machine is always a scam.

This is not true!

If you are about to buy a blueprint from the BUY menu, BEFORE you do that, make sure to check the games to see if it is in one of the games! If it is, keep playing the game until you get the blueprint as it only costs 10k starchrom to finish the game (500 per roll), and you usually get your blueprint before 20 rolls.

For example, the AWS Bingo/Bullseye costs 8k starchrom. However, in one of the games you can roll for it. AND ON TOP OF THAT, you get the WILDFIRE BP too! so EVEN IF it takes you all 20 rolls, it costs 10k starchrom to get those blueprints from the shop anyway. But on top of the AWS and Wildfire blueprints you get from the wish, you get a bunch of other random blueprint frags which are used to enhance other blueprints.

It doesn't end there! Even if you bought the blueprint from the shop menu, you can still play the game and get frags for the same blueprint and enhance it a lot! I did this after buying the AWS Bingo/Bullseye, and got it to 3 stars!

Server Transfer Checklist

  • Make advanced electric rock drill & advanced logging chainsaw (headstart on gathering ores & wood in new season)
  • Materials for normal furnaces (to salt farm for XP)
  • Loot all mystical crates, accessories, and puzzles (you can reloot mystical crates after reset)
  • Buy starchrom crates for energy links (sell gold, silver, etc to merchants to get lots of energy links)
  • Use controllers if you wanna farm mods (You can't farm pro silos in phase 1 - 3)
  • Claim battlepass chests after you transfer, not before, so that you get a headstart on some resources (the chests are the rewards that you get after completing the battlepass)
  • Reset all your memetics so that you get some energy links out of them (which turn into astral sand)
  • Obtain the deviants that you get when crafting items. You won't have the resources to make them after you transfer. (ex: craft vortex gen grenades to get mini wonder)
